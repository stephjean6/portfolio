A Little Bit About Steph

Family

Steph was born in Tacoma, Washington and grew up in a close-knit family of four; she is the youngest of two children.

  • Chuck, Steph's dad
  • Susie, Steph's mom
  • Stacy, Steph's older sister

Childhood

When Steph was a little girl, her dad got a job teaching college in Pennsylvania, so the family lived there during the school year and in Tacoma, Washingon during the summer. The family enjoyed this arrangement because it allowed them to experience different parts of the country.

As a child, Steph enjoyed playing outside with her friends; she particularly liked riding her bike, hiking, making crafts, and swimming.

Steph always felt that the Pacific Northwest was her home; after graduate school, she moved to Gig Harbor.

Education

Attending the University of Virginia, Steph double majored in anthropology and philosophy.

After undergraduate, Steph attended graduate school in Nebraska, but took a leave of absence and became a potter!

Steph values education and has always said that if she wins the lottery, then she would take college courses and do art for fun.

Hobbies

Arts and crafts have always been a big part of Steph's life. She enjoys making pottery and jewelry as well as painting, sewing, and drawing.

Every week, Steph spends time with her family making art. She enjoys taking her children to paint-your-own-pottery shops and local craft shows.
