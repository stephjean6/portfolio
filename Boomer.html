Boomer 101

Boomer's real name is Josh. He is a seven-year-old, special-needs boy who lives in Bellevue, Washington with his family. He was born on a beautiful day in October 2009.

His dad really wanted his legal name to be Boomer, but his mom preferred a more taraditional name. So they agreed that Boomer would be his nickname.

Josh Collier

Josh is a fantastic first-grader. He loves going to school and makes friends easily.

Coach O is his favorite teacher. Coach O, as you can imagine, teaches physical education. Josh LOVES to ride scooters in gym class, and also enjoys running under the parachute. When he plays outside, Boomer enjoys running on the playground and swinging on the playset.

Boomer's Special Needs

Special needs come in a variety of shapes and sizes. Boomer is one of 1000 children worldwide who has Cohen Syndrome. This means that Boomer has a variety of physical disabilities and developmental delays, but those limitations don't stop him from being a cheerful, curious, and friendly little boy.

Boomer's 7th Birthday

Boomer really enjoys playing on his iPad, which he also uses to talk! He plays alphabet and number games, and especially likes to make art. Boomer really enjoys crayons and markers.

Boomer on a Swing!

Boomer's most favorite activity is swinging. He enjoys swinging high and low, fast and slow. He loves porch swings, tire swings, playground swings and hammock swings!
